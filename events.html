Danny Akalepse Album Release

MONDAY APRIL 17, 2017 . 9 PM

Keeping the source material of this mixtape in mind – Impressions of Impressions is a collection of covers and interpretations from that seminal band's catalog – Danny Akalepse has decided to release this cassette in their original home base of Chicago. Dave Mata and Mo Manley will also help hold down the tunes. Easy-going vibes should make way to some classic shakin'.

Jager will be in the house pouring some of their good good.

Brooklyn Sessions

TUESDAY APRIL 25, 2017 . 8 PM

Charles Mantis will be bringing out a trio to perform alongside his pristine compositions on sp404, brass, and a host of other percussion and electronics. If you let Big Gidz' dense sampling and beats lull you into a trance you might miss some of his cutting eloquence on the mic. Best to caffeine up.

I Am Tom Collins

TUESDAY MAY 2, 2017 . 9 PM

Prairie Spirits and Deadbolt bring you I Am Tom Collins. Performing: vocal trio Babe Alon 5 coming with a set of golden standards and reinterpretations of same. American pre and post war vocal melodies in keeping with the night's Americana Theme. Justin Howard will be spinning from his collection of vintage country 45's and 7 inches.
